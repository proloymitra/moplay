Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

Please use our contact form above with "Bug Report" as the subject, or email us directly at tech@playinmo.com. Include as much detail as possible about the issue.

How can I submit my game to PlayInMo?

We welcome game submissions from developers! Please email games@playinmo.com with your game details, or use the contact form with "Game Submission" as the subject.

How do I recover my account?

If you're having trouble accessing your account, please contact our support team at support@playinmo.com with your account details.

Can I advertise on PlayInMo?

Yes! We offer various advertising opportunities. Please contact business@playinmo.com or visit our Advertise With Us page for more information.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and associated data, please contact support@playinmo.com with your request. We'll process it within 48 hours.

What browsers are supported?

PlayInMo works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.
